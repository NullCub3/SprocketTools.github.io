Contributing Decals

To contribute your own decals to the catalog, send them to the website's developer (Colson).
You can find him by joining the Wonder Waffle Discord or the Sprocket Official Discord.

Decals need to meet the following criteria:

- File size cannot exceed 256kb
- Names should be in PascalCase and not exceed 25 characters in length
Send your decals in a .zip file to simplify the integration process.
